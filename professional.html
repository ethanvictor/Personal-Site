Research Intern (Hamilton Trust / CRANN)

Trinity College Dublin / CRANN, Dublin, Ireland

Worked on the project “Quantum Monte Carlo as an Annealing Problem on a Probabilistic Computer” under the supervision of Prof. Stefano Sanvito. Developed a Python-based p-bit emulator for classical and quantum spin models, mapped quantum spin lattices and optimisation problems to classical spin systems, and analysed energy convergence, critical temperatures, metastability, and spin-chain mappings. Investigated probabilistic algorithms on NP-complete optimisation problems and presented findings through technical presentations, a public poster, and a final written report.
